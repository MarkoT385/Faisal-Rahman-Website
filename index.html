Faisal Rahman, Ph.D.

Academician, Healthcare Entrepreneur, Author, Speaker and Consultant on the Global platform. Dr. Rahman is a rare individual who bridges the gap between the academic world and the world of business with particular focus on the healthcare sector.

Business

Principal and Co-Founder of APAC group of healthcare companies. APAC owns, manages, and operates healthcare companies focused on outpatient surgery and physician practice management.

Dr. Rahman serves on the Board of Directors of Munster Surgery Center, APAC Partners LLC, Rahmking Enterprises, the Educational Advisory Board of Health Connect Partners and other organizations.

Partial list of organizations who Dr. Rahman has provided advisory/consulting services include:

  • Universities and hospitals in India
  • Universities and hospitals in China
  • Universities and hospitals in Italy
  • Universities and hospitals in Morocco
  • Universities and hospitals in Jordan
  • Universities and hospitals in France
  • Universities and hospitals in Canada

Special expertise in valuation of healthcare enterprises and advising in sale and partnership negotiations on behalf of physician practices, ambulatory surgery centers and hospitals.

Academia

  • Professor and Founding Dean of The Graham School of Management, Saint Xavier University, Chicago, USA — an AACSB accredited institution.
  • Post-doctoral work at University of Chicago, New York University and University of Wisconsin-Madison.
  • Expert in curriculum design and mentoring students and faculty for the global corporate world.

Dr. Rahman's particular areas of interest and expertise include curriculum design, and delivery for institutions of higher learning as well as efficiency of management in healthcare organizations in both the United States and other countries. He was awarded an Honorary Doctorate in International Management by a consortium of universities in Paris, France.
